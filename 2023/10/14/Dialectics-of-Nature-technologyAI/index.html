一、技术发展与人类社会演变的广义关联

The broad correlation between technological development and the evolution of human society

在1860年的一天，法国的皇帝拿破仑三世在宴请各国国王和贵族的时候，在餐具的准备上采用了不同的处理来彰显自己的显赫地位，当时为诸位来宾准备了精美异常的金银餐具，而为自己选用了一套貌不惊人的铝制餐具。在当时的技术来看铝这类金属的提炼工艺技术较为“先进”，是平民难以企及，大臣贵族也无法支付的昂贵品，以这种方式彰显自己的高贵尊严。在当时看来这种手段确实有所成效，在200多年后的今天来看，铝制品的制作工艺相当成熟，铝制餐具的实用型与尊贵身份早已远不及那些精美的金银餐具。从当今视角看来法国帝王的行为反而是南辕北辙，究其因，是技术的发展改变了社会的基础认知，进而影响社会的发展。

One day in 1860, the French emperor Napoleon III in the feast of kings and nobles, in the preparation of tableware on the use of different treatments to show their eminent status, at that time for the guests to prepare a beautiful and unusual gold and silver tableware, and for their own choice of a set of aluminum tableware does not look amazing. At that time, the technology of aluminum such metal refining process technology is more “advanced”, is difficult to reach the common people, ministers and nobles can not pay for the expensive products, in this way to show their noble dignity. At that time, this means indeed seem to have been effective, in more than 200 years later today, the aluminum production process is quite mature, aluminum tableware practical and noble status has long been far less than those of the exquisite gold and silver tableware. From today’s perspective, the French emperor’s behavior is the opposite, the reason is that the development of technology has changed the basic perception of society, which in turn affects the development of society.

技术的发展对人类的影响

The impact of technological developments on humanity

两次工业革命是技术改变社会的典范，除了技术对于社会生产力的推动，技术发展也促进了社会体量的提升，人口的提升也收到了技术发展的红利，人口的发展也催生出更多的生产力与劳动人口，从而演化出不同的阶级，工人阶级的庞大体量与阶级间的矛盾是对于社会发展的重要推动力。

The two industrial revolutions are examples of technological change in society. In addition to the impetus of technology on social productivity, technological development also promotes the enhancement of social volume, and the enhancement of population also receives the dividends of technological development, and the development of population also gives rise to more productivity and labor force, which leads to the evolution of different classes, and the huge volume of the working class and the contradiction between the classes is an important impetus for social development.

恩格斯的《国民经济学批判大纲》被马克思誉为“批判经济学范畴的天才大纲”，在《大纲》中恩格斯揭露了马尔塞斯人口论的虚伪性，指出了科学技术的重要性。恩格斯批判了马尔萨斯关于人口过剩理论的荒谬错误观点，马尔萨斯认为土地生长是按照1+2+3+4+5+6的线性增长的，人口却是按照1+2+4+8+16+32的指数增长，但是受限于土地增速是稳定且缓慢，导致依赖土地资源的粮食资源也是稳定且缓慢的增长但是人口增长速度却是呈爆炸式增长，使得物质粮食的生产速度远远跟不上人口的爆炸式增长。这一想法对于当时发展的历史现状相悖。恩格斯批判了马尔萨斯关于人口论的荒谬，那就是马尔萨斯忽略了一个重要前提，那就是影响生产力发展的最显著的因素——科学技术，恩格斯指出“在最普通的情况下，科学也是按几何级数发展的[1]。”

Engels’ Outline of the Critique of National Economics was hailed by Marx as “a genius outline of the scope of critical economics”, in which Engels exposed the hypocrisy of Malthus’s theory of population and pointed out the importance of science and technology. Engels criticized Malthus’s absurd and erroneous views on the theory of overpopulation, in which Malthus believed that the growth of land grows linearly according to 1+2+3+4+5+6, while the population grows exponentially according to 1+2+4+8+16+32, but the growth rate of the land is limited by the fact that the growth rate of the land is stable and slow, which results in the food resources relying on the land resources also grows steadily and slowly, but the growth rate of the population is explosive, which makes the population grow faster and faster. However, the population growth rate was explosive, making the production of material food far from keeping up with the explosive growth of the population. This idea was contrary to the historical realities of the time. Engels criticized the absurdity of Malthus’s theory of population, that is, Malthus ignored an important premise, that is, the most significant factors affecting the development of productive forces - science and technology, Engels pointed out that “in the most ordinary cases, science is also developed in geometric progression [1]. “

在《英国工人阶级状况》一书中，恩格斯对技术的研究进一步加深，并总结出技术的几点作用：一、技术使得生产方式发生改变。二、技术发展打破了原有的封闭状态和生产方式。恩格斯基于当时英国工人的生活现状进行考察，认识到资本主义条件下大规模的使用，对工人的生命健康和心理健康造成了极大的摧残，在资本家的无限度逐利下，许多工人对最基础的温饱问题都难以解决。在经济、生活和精神层次对工人造成了巨大的影响，而对于技术在资本主义社会中的影响，产生了技术异化的现象，恩格斯结合技术在社会结构中寻找方案，为后续的研究奠定了基础，也明确出技术对于社会整体的影响不是单方面的，结合后续的《德意志意识形态》、《共产党宣言》、《反杜林论》的思考调研，最终在《自然辩证法》中给出科学技术的全面概括总结，并在其中提出：科学技术是社会革命的最高力量，科学技术直接或间接驱动上层建筑的变革。一方面，通过科学技术改良生产工具，提高生产力、转变经济发展结构和经济发展模式，从而推动整个人类社会的横向发展，同时也改善人类的生活方式和发展方式:另一方面，科学技术不光影响着生产关系的变革，同时还影响着构建于生产关系之上的上层建筑对政治、经济、文化、社会、生态等方方面面，都产生直接和深刻的影响[2]。科学技术是社会发展的最高力量。

In The Condition of the Working Class in England, Engels further deepened his study of technology and summarized several roles of technology: i. Technology makes changes in the mode of production. Second, technological development breaks the original closed state and mode of production. Engels examined the living conditions of British workers at that time based on the current situation, and realized that the large-scale use of capitalist conditions had caused great destruction to the life and health and mental health of workers, and that under the unlimited profit-seeking of capitalists, many workers had difficulty in solving the problem of subsistence at the most basic level. In the economic, life and spiritual level of the workers caused great impact, and for the impact of technology in capitalist society, produced the phenomenon of technological alienation, Engels combined with technology in the social structure to find a program for the subsequent research laid the foundation for the subsequent research, but also made clear that the impact of technology on the society as a whole is not unilateral, combined with the subsequent “German Ideology”, “Communist Manifesto”, “The Anti-Dühring Theory of thinking and research, and finally in the Dialectics of Nature to give a comprehensive overview of science and technology summarized, and in which it is proposed that: science and technology is the supreme force of social revolution, science and technology, directly or indirectly drive the change of the superstructure. On the one hand, through science and technology to improve the means of production, increase productivity, change the structure of economic development and economic development model, thereby promoting the horizontal development of the entire human society, but also improve the way of life and development of human beings: on the other hand, science and technology not only affects the change of the relations of production, but also affects the construction of the relations of production above the superstructure of the political, economic, cultural, social, ecological, etc., all have a direct and indirect impact on the development of human society, and the development of human society. In all aspects of politics, economy, culture, society, ecology, etc., have a direct and profound impact [2]. Science and technology is the highest force of social development.

人类对技术的需求和利用

Human demand and utilization of technology

马克思在资本论中有句话：“有适当的利润，资本就会非常胆壮起来。只要有10％的利润，它就会到处被人使用；有20％，就会活泼起来；有50％，就会引起积极的冒险；有100%，就会使人不顾一切法律；有300％，就会使人不怕犯罪，甚至不怕绞首的危险。” 这充分说明了资本的逐利性，在两次工业革命中技术的发展及部分资本主义的原始积累的完成使得资本主义逐步走向舞台的中心，收到科学技术发展红利的资本主义的推动下，科学技术的发展也逐步增高，当资本的生产资料达到一定瓶颈时，提升科学技术带来的利润要远高于以当前技术扩展体量的收益时，具有逐利行的资本主义会是科学技术发展的强有力的推动者。

Marx has a saying in Capital: “With a proper profit, capital becomes very bold. With 10 per cent profit, it is used everywhere; with 20 per cent, it comes alive; with 50 per cent, it gives rise to active risk-taking; with 100 per cent, it makes one defy all laws; with 300 per cent, it makes one not afraid of committing a crime, not even of the danger of being hanged.” This fully explains the profit-seeking nature of capital, in the two industrial revolutions in the development of technology and part of the completion of capitalism’s primitive accumulation makes capitalism gradually to the center of the stage, received the scientific and technological development of the dividends of the capitalism of the promotion of science and technology development has also been gradually increased, when the capital of the means of production to reach a certain bottleneck, to enhance the science and technology to bring profits to be much higher than the current volume of technology expansion When the means of production of capital reaches a certain bottleneck, the profit brought by the upgrading of science and technology is much higher than the gain of expanding the volume of the current technology, the capitalism with profit-seeking behavior will be a strong promoter of the development of science and technology.

除了资本主义对于科学技术的推动外，人类自身的认知活动、对环境的保护、对未知领域的探索和生活质量的追求都在不同程度上对科学技术有不同程度的需求。技术的发展也反过来影响人类的需求和利用，技术带来的好处为社会的发展提供了不同的思路与方向，技术进步能够把人类从繁重恶劣的劳动环境中解放出来，实现生产过程的电气化、自动化、智能化，提高生产效率，提高社会生产力的水平，创造出无穷无尽的财富，消灭饥饿和贫困，战胜各种各样的自然灾害，保障人类的生存安全，战胜各种各样的疾病，保障人类的身心健康。

In addition to capitalism’s promotion of science and technology, mankind’s own cognitive activities, protection of the environment, exploration of unknown areas and the pursuit of quality of life are all, to varying degrees, in different degrees of science and technology have different degrees of demand. The development of technology also affects human needs and utilization in turn, and the benefits brought by technology provide different ideas and directions for the development of society. Technological progress can liberate human beings from the heavy and harsh labor environment, realize the electrification, automation, and intelligence of the production process, improve the efficiency of production, raise the level of social productivity, create endless wealth, eliminate hunger and poverty, defeat It will eliminate hunger and poverty, overcome all kinds of natural disasters and ensure the survival of human beings, and overcome all kinds of diseases and ensure the physical and mental health of human beings.

科学技术本质上就是生产力，在人类文明社会发展和更替的进程中，其中离不开一个基础因素，也是最重要的一个因素，那就是科学技术，科学技术是最高意义上的革命性力量，这是恩格斯科技思想的核心要义。科技进步对人类文明和社会制度的发展，有着非同寻常的催化作用，随着科技的不断发展，必定会进一步释放生产力。人类对于技术的利用最先体现在人的生产生活实践中，而后进一步影响生产关系，最终改变社会阶级，社会意识，以及社会形态。

Science and technology are essentially productive forces, and in the process of the development and replacement of human civilization and society, one of the fundamental and most important factors is science and technology, which is a revolutionary force of the highest significance, which is the core essence of Engels’ ideas on science and technology. Scientific and technological progress has an extraordinary catalytic effect on the development of human civilization and social system, and with the continuous development of science and technology, it will surely further unleash the productive forces. The utilization of technology by human beings is first manifested in the practice of human production and life, and then further affects the relations of production, ultimately changing the social class, social consciousness, and social form.

技术与人之间的关系

The relationship between technology and people

在技术的社会的发展初始过程中，技术的发展相对较慢，当工业革命发生时，技术的突破大幅度提高了社会的生产力，反而促进了工业革命地区内的社会变革，进一步将影响辐射到全国乃至全世界，对于人类社会整体有着强劲的推动力。受限于全球的国家间的纷争，不同国家间的竞争关系也为技术的井喷式爆发提供了社会基础，典型的例子为美苏间冷战时期的各类技术的井喷发展，在这期间核武器的研发、太空竞赛、计算机科学的理论基础、军事技术的快速发展和情报站等。科学技术与人类社会间是相互促进共同发展的协同关系，当然也不完全是线性的协同关系，在整体上是螺旋上升的趋势，其中也不乏由于错误的社会关系对技术的抑制，也有不少技术对人类社会有不小的阻力。

In the initial process of the development of technological society, the development of technology is relatively slow, when the industrial revolution occurred, technological breakthroughs greatly increased the productivity of society, but to promote social change in the industrial revolution area, and further radiation of the impact to the whole country and even the world, for the human society as a whole has a strong impetus. Restricted by the global interstate strife, the competitive relationship between different countries also provides a social basis for the eruption of technology, a typical example is the Cold War between the United States and the Soviet Union during the eruption of various types of technological development, the development of nuclear weapons, the space race, the theoretical basis of computer science, the rapid development of military technology, and the intelligence station, and so on. The synergistic relationship between science and technology and human society is one of mutual promotion and co-development, but of course it is not entirely a linear synergistic relationship; on the whole, it is an upward spiral, and there is no lack of inhibition of technology due to erroneous social relations, and there are many technologies that have a considerable amount of resistance to human society.

我国在前几次工业革命中属于后来者，受到历史问题的制约而导致前几次工业革命的落后。经过几代领导人的不懈努力与毕生奋斗后，在第三次的信息革命中，我国逐渐在工业革命的浪潮中追赶上来。在21世纪，信息技术全面的推进，科技革新周期短、速度快、范围广，在创造物质、精神财富的同时，也造成了可观的技术与人、自然、社会之间的矛盾，比如科技异化与人的异化，自然生态与社会环境的失衡，科技对人文或伦理的冲击等等。自21世纪以来，科学技术哲学研究持续关注科技与人、自然、社会的关系，并形成中国语境下的STS研究模式。尽管已有一定影响力，但研究机构与个体的协作仍需加强。科学技术哲学研究具有持久性和现实性特征，关键词聚类的时间线图谱显示多数关注点已被持久关注且随时间显示出阶段性特征。研究也从理论研究向实践探索转变，因为科技发展的目标指向实效性，这使其对理论和社会发展具有双重价值。此外，科学技术哲学研究正在向具体化和多样化发展，扩展至信息哲学、产业哲学、生物学哲学等领域[3]。

Our country was a latecomer to the previous industrial revolutions, and was constrained by historical problems that caused it to lag behind in the previous industrial revolutions. After the unremitting efforts and lifelong struggle of several generations of leaders, China gradually caught up with the industrial revolution in the third information revolution. In the 21st century, the comprehensive advancement of information technology, the short cycle, fast speed and wide range of scientific and technological innovation, while creating material and spiritual wealth, has also caused considerable contradictions between technology and human beings, nature and society, such as scientific and technological alienation and the alienation of human beings, the imbalance between the natural ecology and the social environment, and the impact of science and technology on the humanities or ethics, and so on. Since the 21st century, the study of philosophy of science and technology has continued to focus on the relationship between science and technology and man, nature and society, and has formed the STS research model in the Chinese context. Despite its influence, the collaboration between research institutions and individuals still needs to be strengthened. Philosophy of science and technology research is characterized by durability and relevance, and the timeline mapping of keyword clustering shows that most of the concerns have been persistently focused on and have shown stage characteristics over time. Research has also shifted from theoretical research to practical exploration, as the goal of scientific and technological development points to effectiveness, which makes it of dual value for theory and social development. In addition, research in the philosophy of science and technology is becoming more specific and diversified, expanding into areas such as the philosophy of information, the philosophy of industry, and the philosophy of biology [3].

在2022年新一轮科技革命和产业变革更加深入，前沿技术继续取得重大进步。以中美科技博弈加剧为标志，科技竞争与国家安全的融合不断加深，美国率领盟友加紧在关键原材料、数字基础设施、科技人才、技术规则及标准制定等方面系统地建立起结构性优势，以有利于美国的方式塑造竞争。我国科技发展面临的外部压力增大，环境更加复杂，维护科技安全与发展的挑战增加。[4]在2022年的前沿科技趋势展望一文中，前沿科学技术的交叉融合趋势显著，新的成果大量涌现，在基础研究中有革命性发展，在微观粒子领域、量子计算机和天体物理领域内有不同程度的发展。应用基础领域内几何深度模型的推演速度提升，蛋白质结构预测速度提升60倍，人工智能协助求解复杂量子方程，器官研究和大脑神经活动的深入发展。应用研究领域内，以RNA为核心生物计算机能带来划时代的改变，对基因突变的随机性认知发生改变，柏林工业大学的DNN完全折叠神经网络有望超过人脑称为最强大的神经网络，芬兰的新超导量子比特技术发展为量子计算机的重大基础，韩国的复合电极促进可穿戴电子设备的发展。以科技为核心的竞争将推动世界多极化，科技地缘格局将出现高度复杂性和不确定性。当今的技术对于人的发展的影响是十分巨大的，我国在经过几代人的艰苦奋斗后，虽已在部分领域内取得领域核心地位，但发展依旧受限，科技覆盖范围尚不完善，部分领域仍然处于空白阶段，后续的科研工作还有很长的路要走。

In 2022 a new round of scientific and technological revolution and industrial change has become more profound, and cutting-edge technologies continue to make significant progress. Marked by the intensification of the US-China science and technology game, the fusion of science and technology competition and national security has deepened, with the US leading its allies to step up efforts to systematically establish structural advantages in key raw materials, digital infrastructure, scientific and technological talent, technical rules and standard-setting, etc., and to shape the competition in a way that favors the US. China’s science and technology development is facing increased external pressure, a more complex environment, and increased challenges in maintaining science and technology security and development. [4] In the article Frontier Science and Technology Trends Outlook for 2022, there is a significant trend of cross-fertilization of frontier science and technology, with a proliferation of new results, revolutionary developments in basic research, and developments of varying degrees within the fields of microscopic particles, quantum computers, and astrophysics. In the field of applied fundamentals, there is an increase in the speed of geometric depth modeling, a 60-fold increase in the speed of protein structure prediction, artificial intelligence to assist in solving complex quantum equations, and a deepening of organ research and neurological activity in the brain. Within the field of applied research, RNA-centered biocomputers can bring about epochal changes, the perception of the randomness of genetic mutations changes, the DNN fully folded neural network at the Technical University of Berlin is expected to overtake the human brain as the most powerful neural network, the new superconducting quantum bit technology in Finland develops into a major foundation for quantum computers, and composite electrodes in South Korea promote the development of wearable electronic devices. Competition centered on science and technology will drive the world’s multipolarity, and the geopolitical landscape of science and technology will see a high degree of complexity and uncertainty. Today’s technology for the development of human influence is very great, China after several generations of hard work, although has been in some areas of the field to achieve the field core position, but the development is still limited, the coverage of science and technology is not yet perfect, part of the field is still in the blank stage, the follow-up of the scientific research work there is a long way to go.

人工智能技术的发展对人类的影响

The impact of the development of artificial intelligence technology on humans

人工智能（Artificial Intelligence），也称为机器智能，指由人造出来的机器所代表的智能。通常的人工智能是指依赖于计算机技术实现人类智能的技术。安德烈亚斯·卡普兰和迈克尔·海恩莱因将人工智能定义为“系统正确解释外部数据，从这些数据中学习，并利用这些知识通过灵活适应实现特定目标和任务的能力”。人工智能被定义为模仿人类与人类思维相关的认知功能的机器或计算机，如学习和解决问题等。在计算机领域内，人工智能是感知环境并采取措施以提高其成功机会。目前人工智能的主要组成部分为专家系统、启发式解决问题、自然语言处理和计算机视觉。

Artificial Intelligence (AI), also known as machine intelligence, refers to the intelligence represented by machines that are built by man. Typically AI refers to computer technology that relies on computer technology to realize human intelligence. Andreas Kaplan and Michael Heinlein define Artificial Intelligence as “the ability of a system to correctly interpret external data, to learn from that data, and to use that knowledge to achieve specific goals and tasks through flexible adaptation”. Artificial intelligence is defined as machines or computers that mimic human cognitive functions associated with human thought, such as learning and problem solving. Within the field of computing, AI is the ability to perceive the environment and take steps to improve its chances of success. Currently the main components of AI are expert systems, heuristic problem solving, natural language processing and computer vision.

基于以上四个不同的领域，人工智能技术的不断累计发展， AI的核心问题包括建构能够跟人类似甚至超越人类的推理、知识、计划、学习、交流、感知、移动 、移物、使用工具和操控机械的能力等。目前的人工智能技术在影像识别、语言分析、棋类游戏领域内以及单方面达到了超越人类的水平。得益于数学理论、逻辑推演、仿生学、认知心理学、概率论和经济学等领域的进步AI的技术也在逐步探索完善当中。

Based on the above four different fields, the continuous cumulative development of artificial intelligence technology, the core issues of AI include the construction of reasoning, knowledge, planning, learning, communication, perception, movement, shifting objects, the use of tools and manipulation of machinery, etc., which are similar to or even beyond the ability of human beings. Current AI technology has surpassed human levels in the fields of image recognition, language analysis, board games, and unilaterally. Thanks to advances in the fields of mathematical theory, logical deduction, bionics, cognitive psychology, probability theory and economics, AI technology is gradually being explored and perfected.

在《人工智能与中国产业高质量发展———基于对产业升级与产业结构优化的实证分析》报告中，从数据入手分析了人工智能技术在我国对产业发展的影响。其研究结论为人工智能对三次产业升级与产业结构优化影响效果均具有差异性，从全国范围看，人工智能技术可以促进制造业产业升级，并在不控制时间因素下，可以促进农业、服务业产业升级。对产业结构合理化影响不显著，对产业结构高级化具有显著负向影响。从不同区域看，人工智能技术对农业、制造业没有显著影响;但是，对西部地区服务业升级具有显著正向影响，对长三角及东南沿海地区服务业升级具有显著负向影响。此外，人工智能技术对东北地区、京津冀及中部地区的产业结构合理化水亚具有显著正向影响，对东北地区、西部地区的产业结构高级化水平也具有显著正向影响，而对京津冀及中部地区、长三角及东南沿海地区的产业结构高级化水平具有显著负向影响[5]。

In the report “Artificial Intelligence and High-Quality Development of Chinese Industries - Based on Empirical Analysis of Industrial Upgrading and Industrial Structure Optimization”, it analyzes the impact of AI technology on industrial development in China from the data. Its research concludes that the effect of artificial intelligence on the three industrial upgrading and industrial structure optimization impacts are all differentiated, and that on a national scale, artificial intelligence technology can promote the upgrading of manufacturing industries and, without controlling for the time factor, can promote the upgrading of agricultural and service industries. It has a non-significant effect on the rationalization of industrial structure and a significant negative effect on the advanced industrial structure. From different regions, AI technology has no significant impact on agriculture and manufacturing industries; however, it has a significant positive impact on service industry upgrading in the western region, and a significant negative impact on service industry upgrading in the Yangtze River Delta and the southeastern coastal region. In addition, AI technology has a significant positive impact on the rationalization of the industrial structure of the Northeast, Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei and central regions of the water sub, and also has a significant positive impact on the level of industrial structure advanced in the Northeast and western regions, while it has a significant negative impact on the level of industrial structure advanced in Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei and central regions, the Yangtze River Delta and Southeast Coastal region [5].

人类对人工智能技术的需求和利用

Human demand and utilization of artificial intelligence technology

人工智能技术的需求源于人类对技术的不断追求和探索。人工智能技术的发展可以帮助人类解决很多问题，例如自动化生产、自动驾驶、医疗诊断等等。此外，人工智能技术还可以帮助人类更好地理解和处理大量的数据，从而提高生产效率和决策的准确性。随着计算机的快速发展，现代计算机网络产生的数据量大幅度提升，面对巨大的数据时，计算机的快速计算优势便展现出来了，随着计算机对生产活动的加速带来了巨大的利润空间和技术需求，在资本主义的逐利性本质的推动下，人工智能的快速发展已成必然之势。

The demand for artificial intelligence technology stems from mankind’s continuous pursuit and exploration of technology. The development of AI technology can help human beings solve many problems, such as automated production, automatic driving, medical diagnosis and so on. In addition, AI technology can help humans better understand and process large amounts of data, thus improving productivity and the accuracy of decision-making. With the rapid development of computers, the amount of data generated by modern computer networks has increased dramatically, in the face of the huge amount of data, the computer’s fast computational advantages will be shown, with the acceleration of the computer on the production activities brought about by the huge profit margins and technological needs, in the nature of the capitalist profit-seeking nature of the drive, the rapid development of artificial intelligence has become an inevitable trend.

人工智能的快速发展为世界带来了遍历与问题，相较于以前的理论指导实践的慢速发展模式，现今的AI领域内的伦理领域的理论发展尚未完善，在社会道德水平尚未追赶到技术发展时伦理问题为技术的发展提出了更深刻的要求。生产力的提升时实现人全面发展的前提条件，而AI技术的发展能削弱旧式分工、增加闲暇时间为人全面发展提供保障。

The rapid development of Artificial Intelligence (AI) has brought the world experiences and problems, compared with the previous slow development mode of theory-guided practice, the theoretical development of the ethical field in the field of AI has not yet been perfected, and the ethical issues put forward more profound requirements for the development of technology when the moral level of the society has not yet caught up with the development of technology. Productivity enhancement is a prerequisite for the realization of comprehensive human development, and the development of AI technology can weaken the old-style division of labor and increase leisure time to provide a guarantee for the comprehensive development of human beings.

在人工智能发展的途中，要以道驭技，保持人对技术的主体地位、要因时而动，促进教育与生产劳动深度融合、要系统思考，赋能人全面发展的洞察力和想象力[6]。正由于这些新的需求为人工智能的技术发展提供了新的方向指导。

On the way to the development of artificial intelligence, it is necessary to harness technology with the Tao to maintain the subjective position of human beings over technology, to move with the times to promote the deep integration of education and productive labor, and to think in a systematic way to empower human beings to develop insight and imagination in a comprehensive way [6]. It is because of these new needs for the technical development of artificial intelligence provides a new direction to guide.

人工智能技术与人之间的关系

The relationship between AI technology and people

人工智能技术的发展使未来社会变得越来越难以预测和管理。当人开始考虑人工智能技术对人的可持续性发展和个人自我实现的可能作用时其实是在回应人工智能技术所带来的两种力量推力和拉力。推力是基于技术发展所带来的需求和焦虑，拉力是对未来美好的生活期待与愿景。人正是在审视人工智能所具备的推力和拉力过程中，寻求人的全面发展的可能路径。

The development of AI technology is making the future of society increasingly difficult to predict and manage. When one begins to consider the possible role of AI technology for human sustainability and individual self-realization one is actually responding to two forces push and pull brought about by AI technology. The push is based on the needs and anxieties brought about by technological development, and the pull is the expectation and vision of a better life in the future. It is in the process of examining the push and pull that AI possesses that human beings seek a possible path to comprehensive human development.

人工智能作为独立于其他实体产业的新型领域，对未来带来的影响是不确定的，目前对于人工智能的研究在原始的模拟人的智能行为的基础上转换为大幅度智能化地提高效率的处理事物的需求。人工智能的发展是一个动态的过程，既包括带着明确目标进行开发，也包括收到数据信息提出新的要求才开始的。在人工智能的发展过程中，科学家们会根据人类的需求和问题来设计和开发相应的算法和模型，然后再通过大量的数据训练和完善这些算法和模型。因此，人工智能技术的发展是一个不断迭代、不断优化的过程。

Artificial Intelligence, as a new field independent of other physical industries, is uncertain about the impact it will have on the future, and current research on AI is converting from the original simulation of intelligent human behavior to the need to process things in a substantially more intelligent and efficient manner. The development of artificial intelligence is a dynamic process, which includes both development with a clear goal and the receipt of data and information to put forward new requirements before starting. During the development of AI, scientists design and develop algorithms and models according to human needs and problems, and then train and refine these algorithms and models with a large amount of data. Therefore, the development of AI technology is a process of continuous iteration and optimization.

人工智能技术的发展给人类带来了很多便利和机遇，但同时也存在许多潜在的风险和挑战。一方面，人工智能技术可以提高生产效率、优化资源配置、改善生活质量，为人类创造更多的价值；另一方面，它也可能导致部分岗位的消失、数据安全风险、道德伦理问题等负面影响。因此，我们需要辩证看待人工智能技术与人之间的关系，既要充分利用其优势，又要防范其潜在风险，以实现人与机器的和谐共生。

The development of artificial intelligence technology has brought many conveniences and opportunities to mankind, but there are also many potential risks and challenges. On the one hand, AI technology can increase productivity, optimize resource allocation, improve quality of life, and create more value for human beings; on the other hand, it may also lead to the disappearance of some jobs, data security risks, moral and ethical issues, and other negative impacts. Therefore, we need to take a dialectical view of the relationship between AI technology and human beings, making full use of its advantages while guarding against its potential risks, in order to realize the harmonious coexistence of human beings and machines.

技术的优势

Advantages of technology

技术可以极大地提高生产效率、改善生活质量，例如医学技术的发展使得许多疾病得以治愈，互联网技术让人们能够随时随地获取信息。人工智能技术的优势在于其能够快速处理大量的数据，并从中提取出有用的信息。这可以帮助企业更好地了解客户需求、优化产品设计、提高生产效率等。此外，人工智能技术还可以通过机器学习和深度学习等算法，不断提升自身的智能水平，从而更好地适应不同的场景和应用需求。另外，人工智能技术还可以帮助人们更好地理解和分析复杂的问题，并提供更好的解决方案。例如，在医疗领域，人工智能技术可以通过分析大量的病例数据，帮助医生更好地诊断疾病、制定治疗方案等。在金融领域，人工智能技术可以通过分析市场数据、预测股票价格等，帮助投资者做出更明智的投资决策。人工智能技术还可以帮助企业实现自动化流程，提高工作效率和准确性。在制造业中，人工智能技术可以通过机器人自动化生产线上的操作，减少人工干预和错误率。在客服领域，人工智能技术可以通过自然语言处理和语音识别等技术，实现自动化客服服务，提高客户满意度和忠诚度。

Technology can greatly increase productivity and improve the quality of life, for example, the development of medical technology has made it possible to cure many diseases, and Internet technology allows people to access information anytime and anywhere. The advantage of artificial intelligence technology is its ability to quickly process large amounts of data and extract useful information from it. This can help companies better understand customer needs, optimize product design, improve productivity, and so on. In addition, AI technology can continuously improve its intelligence through algorithms such as machine learning and deep learning, so as to better adapt to different scenarios and application needs. In addition, AI technology can help people better understand and analyze complex problems and provide better solutions. For example, in the medical field, AI technology can help doctors better diagnose diseases and develop treatment plans by analyzing a large amount of case data. In the financial field, AI technology can help investors make smarter investment decisions by analyzing market data and predicting stock prices. AI technology can also help companies automate processes to improve efficiency and accuracy. In manufacturing, AI technology can reduce human intervention and error rates by automating operations on production lines with robots. In the field of customer service, AI technology can automate customer service and improve customer satisfaction and loyalty through technologies such as natural language processing and speech recognition.

人工智能技术对劳动者就业环境存在正向冲击，城镇化水平提升与劳动要素收入增加是人工智能技术影响就业环境的作用路径，与中西部地区、省会及以上城市、智能设施水平较低城市相比，人工智能技术对劳动者就业环境的改善作用在东部及东北地区、普通城市、智能设施水平较高城市更为显著:进一步研究发现，人工智能技术对不同维度就业环境的作用呈现非对等性，其对保障环境的作用较为明显而对市场环境的正向影响不突出:从长期来看，人工智能技术能够持续推动劳动者就业环境改善，且这一促进作用可能更为明显[8]。

There is a positive impact of AI technology on the employment environment of workers, and the improvement of urbanization level and the increase of labor factor income are the paths through which AI technology affects the employment environment. Compared with the central and western regions, provincial capitals and above cities, and cities with lower levels of intelligent facilities, the improvement of the employment environment of workers by AI technology is more significant in the eastern and northeastern regions, ordinary cities, and cities with higher levels of intelligent facilities. Significant: further research found that the role of AI technology on different dimensions of the employment environment shows non-reciprocity, its role in the protection of the environment is more obvious and the positive impact on the market environment is not prominent: in the long term, AI technology can continue to promote the improvement of the employment environment for workers, and this facilitating effect may be more obvious [8].

技术的弊端

The drawbacks of technology

技术也可能带来负面影响，例如工业化进程中的环境污染，互联网时代的信息过载导致人们过度依赖技术，甚至出现网络成瘾等问题。技术的发展也引发了一系列道德和伦理问题，例如生物技术的发展引发了关于生命起源和人类尊严的讨论，人工智能的发展引发了关于机器人权利和人类责任的讨论。技术的发展速度往往超过我们的预期，这可能导致一些未预见到的问题。例如，互联网的发展改变了我们的生活方式，但同时也带来了网络安全、隐私保护等问题。

Technology may also bring negative impacts, such as environmental pollution in the process of industrialization, and information overload in the Internet era, leading to overdependence on technology and even problems such as Internet addiction. The development of technology has also given rise to a series of moral and ethical issues, such as the development of biotechnology, which has triggered discussions on the origin of life and the dignity of human beings, and the development of artificial intelligence, which has triggered discussions on the rights of robots and the responsibilities of human beings. Technology often develops faster than we expect, which can lead to unforeseen problems. For example, the development of the Internet has changed our way of life, but it has also brought about problems such as cybersecurity and privacy protection.

在AI领域人工智能技术虽然有很多优势，但也存在一些弊端。首先，人工智能技术可能会导致大量的工作岗位被取代，从而引发社会就业问题。例如，在制造业中，机器人自动化生产线上的操作可能会取代大量的工人岗位。其次，人工智能技术还可能带来一些安全隐患和隐私问题。例如，在智能家居领域，如果智能设备被黑客攻击，可能会导致家庭和个人的隐私泄露。此外，人工智能技术的应用也可能受到道德和伦理问题的质疑。例如，在医疗领域中，如果人工智能技术的诊断结果与医生的判断不一致，医疗选择问题需要考虑。最后，人工智能技术的发展也需要考虑其对环境和社会的影响。例如，在能源消耗方面，人工智能技术需要大量的电力支持，这可能会增加能源消耗和碳排放量。因此，在推广和应用人工智能技术的过程中，需要综合考虑其优势和弊端，并采取相应的措施来减少其负面影响。

Although artificial intelligence technology in the field of AI has many advantages, it also has some disadvantages. First of all, AI technology may lead to the replacement of a large number of jobs, thus triggering social employment problems. For example, in the manufacturing industry, the operation of robots on automated production lines may replace a large number of worker positions. Second, AI technology may also bring about some security risks and privacy issues. For example, in the smart home sector, if smart devices are hacked, it may lead to privacy leakage of families and individuals. In addition, the application of AI technology may also be questioned by moral and ethical issues. For example, in the medical field, if the diagnostic results of AI technology are inconsistent with the doctor’s judgment, the issue of medical choice needs to be considered. Finally, the development of AI technology also needs to consider its impact on the environment and society. For example, in terms of energy consumption, AI technology requires large amounts of power support, which may increase energy consumption and carbon emissions. Therefore, in the process of promoting and applying AI technology, its advantages and disadvantages need to be considered comprehensively, and appropriate measures need to be taken to minimize its negative impact.

技术的双刃剑特性

The double-edged sword characteristics of technology

技术既有利也有弊，既可以推动社会进步，也可能带来新的问题。这就需要我们在使用技术的同时，也要关注其可能带来的负面影响，并寻找解决办法。在AI领域内的，AI技术的快速发展与方向的不确定性使得发展的相关规定规范产生较慢，在尚未给出合理规范的时候，AI技术带来的可能是薛定谔的魔盒，在尚未明确规范的时候，无法明确出具体的技术好坏。

Technology has both advantages and disadvantages; it can contribute to social progress, but it can also bring new problems. This requires that while we use technology, we also need to pay attention to its possible negative impacts and find solutions. In the field of AI, the rapid development of AI technology and the uncertainty of the direction of the development of the relevant provisions of the norms produced slower, in the reasonable norms have not yet been given, the AI technology brought may be Schrödinger’s magic box, in the clear norms have not yet been clarified, it is impossible to specify the specific technology is good or bad.

与此同时，我们应该意识到，任何人工智能的出现和应用，都会有一定的风险。比如ChatGPT 在高等教育领域使用过程当中的伦理道德问题，一有的利用大数据的漏洞，钻法律的空子，侵犯他人的知识产权。又比如现在会造成学术造假问题，一部分高校学生会依赖ChatGPT 来做“枪手”，完成课程论文甚至是毕业论文的撰写[9]。当前GPT模型的大幅度提升了高技术人员的学习速度与应用能力，对于能力薄弱尚不了解GPT相关技术的人可能是巨大的冲击，在当前工作量有限的情况下，很大几率导致员工收到裁员的风险。

At the same time, we should realize that the emergence and application of any artificial intelligence will have certain risks. For example, ChatGPT in the field of higher education in the use of ethical and moral issues, one of the use of big data loopholes, to exploit the loopholes in the law, infringement of other people’s intellectual property rights. Another example is the problem of academic fraud, some college students will rely on ChatGPT as a “shooter” to complete the course papers or even thesis writing [9]. The current GPT model greatly improves the learning speed and application ability of high-tech personnel, for the weak ability of those who do not yet understand the GPT-related technology may be a huge impact, in the current situation of limited workload, a high probability of leading to the risk of layoffs received by the staff.

总结

summary

在自然辩证法的视角下，科学技术与人类社会的发展是相互影响的，是协同发展进步的关系。从科学技术角度看，新技术的大规模普及会对社会形态造成巨大的影响，尤其是在生产力的大幅度提升时，社会的形态可能会有大幅度的更新乃至产生新的意识形态。在社会意识形态上看，主流社会的号召力和影响力会很大程度的影响技术的发展趋势，面对新技术的接受度和支持度也能推动当前领域技术的深入，进一步加速当前领域内技术的研究。考虑到技术相关规范规定的滞后性发布，使得技术的内容的良恶性大幅度收到用户的反馈，基于此产生的技术负面影响也不可避免，此处需要明确的规定来加快相关技术的规范制定流程以减少进一步的影响，即使技术发展有一定的双面性，综合来看技术的发展是利大于弊的。

From the perspective of the dialectic of nature, science and technology and the development of human society interact with each other in a synergistic relationship of development and progress. From the perspective of science and technology, the large-scale popularization of new technologies will have a great impact on the shape of society, especially when productivity is substantially increased, the shape of society may be substantially renewed and even generate a new ideology. In terms of social ideology, the appeal and influence of the mainstream society will greatly affect the development trend of technology, and the acceptance and support of new technology can also promote the depth of the current field of technology, further accelerating the research of technology in the current field. Considering the lag in the release of technology-related norms and regulations, making the content of the technology of the good and bad substantially received feedback from users, based on which the negative impact of technology is also unavoidable, the need for clear provisions here to speed up the development of technology-related norms to reduce the further impact of the process, even if the development of technology has a certain degree of two-sided, a comprehensive view of the development of technology is more beneficial than detrimental.

人工智能作为最近火热的领域，其中的技术发展具有较多的领域方向，目前的发展仍处于较为年轻的阶段，许多技术尚未形成全面的产业体系，技术的井喷式发展为AI领域的发展带来新鲜的活力，GPT新技术为当前不同的产业带来了巨大的冲击，同时也带来了不小的机遇。面对新的技术不单要从技术本身考虑，也要在教育领域有所展现。教育技术人员应始终关注人工智能技术取得的新进展，了解不断涌现的教育人工智能产品，推动基于人工智能技术的智慧学习环境建设，探索人工智能技术教育应用的方法与策略引领由数据挖掘深度学习等人工智能技术驱动的教育研究促进教育人工智能的良性发展[10]。产业领域可以通过以下几种方式增加对人工智能发展的建设：首先，加强人才培养。人工智能技术的发展需要大量的专业人才，因此，企业可以加强人才培养，提高员工的技能水平，以适应人工智能技术的发展。其次，加强技术研发。企业可以加强自主研发，提高自主创新能力，推动人工智能技术的创新和应用。最后，加强合作交流。企业可以加强与其他企业、高校、科研机构等的合作交流，共同推动人工智能技术的发展。人工智能技术的发展对人类社会的影响是深远的。它可以极大地提高生产效率和质量，改善人们的生活质量。但是，人工智能技术也可能会取代一些工作，导致失业率上升。此外，人工智能技术的安全问题也需要引起关注。未来，人工智能技术可能会更加普及，并应用于更多的领域，如医疗、金融、教育等。同时，我们也需要加强对人工智能技术的监管和管理，确保其安全可控。

Artificial Intelligence as a recent hot field, in which the development of technology has more areas of direction, the current development is still in a relatively young stage, many of the technologies have not yet formed a comprehensive industrial system, the blowout development of technology for the development of the field of AI to bring fresh vitality, the GPT new technology for the current different industries has brought a huge impact, but also brings no small opportunity. Facing the new technology should not only be considered from the technology itself, but also be shown in the field of education. Educational technicians should always pay attention to the new progress made by AI technology, understand the emerging educational AI products, promote the construction of smart learning environment based on AI technology, explore the methods and strategies of educational application of AI technology lead by data mining deep learning and other AI technology-driven educational research to promote the benign development of educational AI [10]. The industrial field can increase the construction of the development of artificial intelligence in the following ways: first, strengthen the training of talents. The development of AI technology requires a large number of professionals, so enterprises can strengthen talent training and improve the skill level of employees to adapt to the development of AI technology. Secondly, strengthen technology research and development. Enterprises can strengthen independent research and development, improve independent innovation ability, and promote the innovation and application of artificial intelligence technology. Finally, strengthen cooperation and exchange. Enterprises can strengthen cooperation and exchange with other enterprises, universities, research institutions, etc., to jointly promote the development of artificial intelligence technology. The development of artificial intelligence technology has a profound impact on human society. It can greatly improve productivity and quality and improve people’s quality of life. However, AI technology may also replace some jobs, leading to an increase in unemployment. In addition, the safety of AI technology needs to be a cause for concern. In the future, AI technology may become more popular and be applied to more fields, such as healthcare, finance and education. At the same time, we also need to strengthen the regulation and management of AI technology to ensure that it is safe and controllable.
