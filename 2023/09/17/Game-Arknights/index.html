鹰角

鹰角网络（HyperGryph，日:ハイパーグリフ）是一家网络游戏研发运营公司，上海鹰角网络科技有限公司（Shanghai HyperGryph Network Technology Co.,Ltd.）旗下。因开发并运营了手机游戏《明日方舟》而闻名。

HyperGryph (Japanese: ハイパーグリフ) is an online game R&D and operation company, owned by Shanghai HyperGryph Network Technology Co., Ltd. Known for developing and operating the mobile game Arknight

鹰角网络主要由海猫和阵面、悠星的朋友创立。于2017年1月24日成立公司，公司经营范围包括从事网络、计算机技术领域内的技术开发、利用信息网络经营游戏产品等。

HyperGryph was mainly founded by 海猫 and friends of 阵面 and 悠星(Yostar). Founded on January 24, 2017, the company’s business scope includes technology development in the field of network and computer technology, and the use of information network to operate game products.

公司的两位创始人（海猫络合物、唯@W）毕业自中国美术学院建筑艺术学院（09届、05届），核心成员中也有不少来自中国美术学院的校友（13届综合设计系、13届美术教育系）。部分成员来自谷歌、无极黑、Cygames等公司。

The two founders of the company (海猫络合物、唯@W) graduated from the School of Architecture and Art of the China Academy of Art (09th and 05th), and many of the core members are alumni from the China Academy of Art (13th Comprehensive Design Department and 13th Art Education Department). Some members come from Google, Massive Black, Cygames and other companies.

目前公司分为两大部门，研发和发行。研发分为策划组、美术组、程序组、文案组和内容组，发行包括运营组等。策划组下有关卡策划和系统策划，主要负责玩家在游戏体验时的具体感受；美术组负责概念设计、角色和场景原画、小人spine、3D战斗场景、UI和演出特效和基建平面装饰元素；程序组负责底层架构、工具效能、战斗机制、角色逻辑、外层系统、中层玩法、本地化和图形渲染；文案组主要负责游戏剧情的文字创作；内容组负责漫画、小说、音乐、动画和PV等在游戏外扩展作品世界观的作品，同时也与各地的优秀艺术家合作（包括角色设计、插画、漫画、动画和小说等）；运营组主要负责处理玩家对游戏的反馈、游戏内活动排期和规划、市场活动和游戏衍生品开发等。

At present, the company is divided into two departments, R&D and distribution. R&D is divided into planning team, art team, program team, copywriting team and content group, and distribution includes operation team. The planning team is mainly responsible for the specific feelings of players during the game experience; The art team is responsible for concept design, character and scene original art, villain spine, 3D battle scenes, UI and performance special effects, and infrastructure graphic decoration elements; The program group is responsible for the underlying architecture, tool effectiveness, combat mechanics, character logic, external system, mid-level gameplay, localization and graphics rendering; The copywriting team is mainly responsible for the text creation of the game plot; The content team is responsible for manga, novels, music, animation, PV and other works that expand the world view of the work outside the game, and also collaborates with outstanding artists around the world (including character design, illustration, manga, animation, novels, etc.); The operation team is mainly responsible for handling player feedback on the game, in-game event scheduling and planning, marketing activities and game derivative development.
